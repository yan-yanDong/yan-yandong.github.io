Research Statement

I am interested in solving online learning optimization problem and finding optimal codes with finite blocklength using mathematical tools. In past, I have studied the problem of combinatorial bandit with a cost for a switch of each selected arm in each round under different feedback settings. I have studied the capacity of a line network in both discrete memoriless scenario and wireless communication scenario when network coding is applied in the network. Also, I justified the optimal codes with finite blocklength for binary symmetric channel among both linear codes and nonlinear codes.

Online learning optimization

  • Combinatorial multi-armed bandit with switching cost

  • Multi-armed bandit with delays on the reward feedback

Network Coding and Network Optimization

  • Network Utility Maximization when network coding is involved
