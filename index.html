About me

I’m a postoc in the School of Science and Engineering (SSE) at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK, Shenzhen).
Prior to being a postdoc in CUHK,Shenzhen, I was a Research Fellow in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE)
at the National University of Singapore (NUS) during Dec 2022 and Dec 2023, where I was hosted by Prof. Vincent Y. F. Tan.
I obtained my Ph.D. in 2022 from School of Science and Engineering (SSE) at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK,Shenzhen)
with Prof. Shenghao Yang as my advisor.
I obtained my Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Jilin University in July 2017.
